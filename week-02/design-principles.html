Proximity

Netflix

netflix.com

We can see the principle of "Proximity" exemplified in this image of the Netflix Home Page because in its body there are 3 well-defined and separate sections that nevertheless have several elements each, but due to their proximity to each other gives a clear idea of their belonging to one or another section

Alignment

BBC News

bbc.com

We can see the principle of "alignment" embodied within this image of the BBC News Home Page because all elements near the edges of the screen respect the same margin or distance, giving a sense of coherence and order, including images, text and buttons

Contrast

Git HUB

github.com

We can see the principle of "contrast" well expressed within this image of the Git HUB Home Page because we can clearly distinguish, thanks to the color differences, the background text, as well as buttons of a form field.
